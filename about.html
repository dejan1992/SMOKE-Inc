About Us

Founded in Berkshire, UK The company was founded by the original directors of Hot Shots Paintball Centre in Newbury, with the idea to manufacture a biodegradable pyrotechnic paint grenade. Whilst trying to bring the paper mache paint grenade to market, the friction smoke grenade was devised and offered as another product line to paintball sites in the UK.

SMOKE INC. is the global leader in the design and manufacture of colored smoke effects for commercial and professional use. Our smoke bombs are the world’s most popular colored smoke devices, and emit the most advanced smoke on the market. Over 20 years of research and development has led SMOKE INC. to be the world’s number one provider of smoke effects across a diverse range of industries. The cool-burning, non-toxic formula and renowned color vibrancy make EG smoke bombs the perfect solution in many professional settings. Our smoke effects help sports teams put on a show to remember. To mass produce high quality smoke grenades it’s essential to retain complete control over the manufacturing process.
